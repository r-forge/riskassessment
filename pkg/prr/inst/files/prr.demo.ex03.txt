<<Risk_Ranger>>
((defi)) This chain is no more that the template
         to be used to reproduce the Risk Ranger
         case, of course in a probabilistic view.
         The different ancestor nodes are described
         in the comment field.
((role)) To be copied for further use
((time)) 2010_11_29
((orig)) Interpretation of [RR] made by Isabelle Albert and Laurent Guillier
((comm))/;/v
   (X1)  [Hazar] it is the hazard severity expressed as a proportion/weight;
   (X2a) [Susc1] some weight for the probability to be ill;
   (X2b) [Susc2] fraction of the susceptible part of the population;
   (X3)  [DCons] frequency of the daily consumption;
   (X4)  [PCons] studied proportion of the population;
   (X5)  [Popul] population size;
   (X6)  [Conta] contamination frequency;
   (X7)  [Proce] process effect on the contamination (??? But how the increase/decrease can be distinguihed with a strictly positive variable ???)
   (X8)  [Recon] frequency of contamination after the process;
   (X9)  [Retai] effect on the contamination of the after process phase;
   (X10) [Proxi] ??? not that clear for me ???;
   (X11) [Prepa] effect of the food preparation (cooking) on the ingested contamination;
   (Y1)  [ProbP] probability that a pathogenic dosis be in a serving;
   (Y2)  [ProbD] probability of disease of a susceptible consumer per day;
   (Y3)  [Disea] disease number in the targetted population;
   (Y4)  [CRisk] comparative risk for the targetted population;
   (Y5)  [CRank] rank associated to the comparative risk
